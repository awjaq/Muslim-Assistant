## Muslim Assistant for Home Assistant

A comprehensive Islamic companion integration for Home Assistant. Your smart home, enriched with the beauty of Islam.

### Features (18 Sensors + 16 Services + Media Player)

- **Prayer Times & Adhan** - Accurate prayer times with 16+ calculation methods, adjustable +/- minutes, and Adhan audio playback
- **Quran Audio** - Listen to Quran recitation from 11+ world-renowned reciters on any speaker (Alexa, Google Home, Sonos)
- **Qibla Direction** - Compass bearing to the Kaaba in Makkah with cardinal direction
- **Holy Quran** - Verse of the day, fetch any surah or ayah with Arabic + English
- **Hijri Calendar** - Islamic calendar date with Arabic names
- **99 Names of Allah** - Daily rotating Asma ul Husna with meanings
- **Daily Duas & Dhikr** - 20+ context-aware supplications throughout the day
- **Inspirational Quotes** - 30 curated quotes from Quran and Hadiths
- **Tasbih Counter** - Digital dhikr counter with customizable targets
- **Ramadan Tracker** - Fasting day counter with Suhoor/Iftar times
- **Mosque Finder** - Find nearby mosques within 5km
- **Halal Food Finder** - Find nearby halal restaurants within 5km
- **Zakat Calculator** - Calculate your annual Zakat obligation
- **Hajj & Umrah Guides** - Step-by-step guides with tips
- **Islamic Greeting Cards** - 12 occasion templates (Eid, Ramadan, Jummah, Wedding, etc.)
- **Makkah Live** - Live stream link from Masjid al-Haram
- **Prayer Requests** - Submit and track prayer requests

### What's New in v2.0

- **Adhan & Quran Audio** - Play Adhan and Quran on Alexa, Google Home, Sonos, or any HA media player
- **11 Quran Reciters** - Mishary Alafasy, Al-Sudais, Abdul Basit, Al-Minshawi, Al-Husary, and more
- **Prayer Time Adjustments** - Fine-tune each prayer time +/- 30 minutes
- **Options Flow** - Reconfigure reciter, Adhan sound, and prayer offsets without removing the integration
- **Media Player Entity** - Dedicated audio player entity for Islamic audio

### Installation

1. Install via HACS (recommended) or copy `custom_components/muslim_assistant` to your config directory
2. Restart Home Assistant
3. Go to **Settings > Devices & Services > Add Integration**
4. Search for "Muslim Assistant"
5. Configure your location and prayer calculation method
6. Optionally configure Quran reciter and prayer adjustments in integration options
